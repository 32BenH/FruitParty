Inspiration Behind the Idea

Bananalyzer is a web-based application that uses image processing to help customers select optimal produce when at the grocery store. The idea revolves around informing shoppers on the status of the produce they are selecting. When customers scan the produce with their phone, data such as days remaining until ripe and days until bad are provided.
